Our scientists research the efficacy of Aim Train

We conducted a randomized study on Aim Train, using crossword puzzles as an active control.

What we did

Our scientists had 4,715 participants complete the study. Half trained with Aim Train, while the rest did online crossword puzzles to control for placebo effects.

What we found

After 10 weeks, the Aim Train group improved more than the crosswords group on an aggregate assessment of cognition.

Next questions

These results are promising, but we need to do more research to determine the connection between improved assessment scores and everyday tasks in participants’ lives. That’s our next focus.
